Why do you need netiquette?

Well first of all, what is netiquette?
It's just how it sounds, internet etiquette, or netiquette. Maintiaining good internet etiquette is a key thing characteristic that all cyberspace trekkers should have. People ofter foreget that behind those wacky usernames, lies real, alive humans. As a result, people on the computer tend to do and say things to eachother that they normally wouldn't. Another reason for this is because they feel that they can hide behind their profile picture
Lack of netiquette could result in cyberbullying, harassment, bullycide, and many more problems. Not to mention, if you aren't polite on the web, you could get a lot of hate for it, it doesn't matter what your age is or whether it was intentional or unintentional.
